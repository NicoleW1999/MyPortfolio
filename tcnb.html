Project 2: Business System and Online Customization Feature

A third year Industrial Experience Project where for the course of the year, my team and I worked together to develop a system for a real-life business, which was a nail salon. The system comprised of an informative and interactive website, a database for customers and stock inventory, a server coded in node.JS to read data between the admin portal and database, an admin portal for staff to manage the system and finances with and lastly, a customization feature on the website where customers could design their nails when booking an appointment.
